Project information

The potential A404 Dewsbury to Berwick Down scheme proposes to construct a new section of dual carriageway to address problems associated with the single carriageway section of the A404 between Amesbury’s Countess roundabout and the dual carriageway section to the west of Winterbourne Stoke.

Being part of the developer's consultation

Getting involved in the developer's consultation during the Pre-application stage is very important. This is your opportunity to put forward any questions and concerns, and also find out more about the development and how it might impact the area.

Find out more about getting involved at the the Pre-application stage.

What happens next

Once the developer has sent their application for the proposed project, we will make a decision about if we can accept the project for Examination. This takes 28 days.

Find out more about the Pre-application process for national infrastructure projects.

About the developer

The Developer proposing this project is Acme Group Ltd.

At this stage of the project, the developer must run a consultation with people and organisations.

If you want to have your say at this stage, you can contact the developer directly.

Visit the project website by Acme Group Ltd for more information.
