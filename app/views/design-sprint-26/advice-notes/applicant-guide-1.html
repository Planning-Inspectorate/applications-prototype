Pre-application service for applicants

The Planning Inspectorate offers a service for applicants at the Pre-application stage of the Planning Act 2008 process.

The service is free and is designed to help applicants in planning and carrying out their Pre-application duties.

See our short introduction on the service from the Planning Inspectorate’s former Director of Major Casework, Mark Southgate.
