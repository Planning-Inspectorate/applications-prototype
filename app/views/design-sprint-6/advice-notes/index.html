Advice notes

Advice notes inform industry and members of the public about a range of planning issues throughout the planning application process under the Planning Act 2008 (PA2008).

Advice notes include:

  • guidance on what to include in an application
  • how the process works

Advice notes

One: Local impact reports (version 2, last updated April 2012)

Advice on the creation of Local Impact Reports (LIR) and includes how it works, the timetable to operate by and what to include in the LIR.

Two: The role of local authorities in the development consent process (version one, published February 2015)

Advice on the role of local authorities at different stages of the Planning Act 2008 process. This includes an overview of the process, the local authority’s role and how to get in touch at various stages.

Three: Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) consultation and notification (version 7, republished August 2017)

This advice explains how the Planning Inspectorate identifies consultation bodies to be notified under Regulation 11 of The Infrastructure Planning (Environmental Impact Assessment) Regulations 2017 (the ‘EIA Regulations’).
